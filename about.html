About us

With the development of the Internet, the prosperous e-commerce business competition has gradually become more fierce, and the companies of commodity sellers need to deal with more than providing low prices and maintaining good product quality. More people start to show their products to more people through diversified promotion methods, in order to increase the popularity and sales of the products. Due to the increasing exposure demand of e-commerce, the intelligent promotion system "JXZShopping" developed by SMART BIG DATA LTD in the United Kingdom was born. JXZShopping is a professional e-commerce promotion platform, mainly to help some companies improve their rankings on the e-commerce platform, increase sales and exposure, so that more people can see and buy more cost-effective products. Since the birth of JXZShopping's business model, JXZShopping's business partners have exceeded 500,000+, and the cumulative business value created for partners has exceeded 2 billion US dollars. Now JXZShopping has launched a membership system with no deposit and no initial fee. You only need to register a real-name account to become a user of the platform. In this way, each of us can easily make money with just a mobile phone. More and more teams and individual user supporters have achieved a more perfect life through JXZShopping.
